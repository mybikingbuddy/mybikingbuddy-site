Your ride goes beyond the leaderboard. The numbers don’t tell the whole story.

Upload your ride summary and describe what was going on during the ride. MyBikingBuddy uses your stats and your own words to explain what actually happened, plus how this ride compares to your recent rides.

How it works

  • Upload: Add a screenshot of your Peloton ride summary.
  • Add context: Tell us anything that mattered, tired legs, poor sleep, a recovery day, soreness, injury, or just a great day.
  • Your ride, put into words: not just numbers anymore, whether you pushed hard, took a recovery ride, or were riding through soreness or even a small injury.

You do not need to link your Peloton account. After your ride, take a photo and we’ll handle the rest.
